# Desolate Forest Landscape Art

## Template

```
A lone [SUBJECT] stands silhouetted in a stark, barren forest under a pale sky. The foreground features a surreal, undulating landscape of black and white stripes, resembling water or distorted ground, with a small, reflective pool of water. The art style is reminiscent of a linocut or woodblock print, with heavy contrast and graphic detail. The overall [MOOD] is conveyed through a limited [COLOR_SCHEME] of deep blacks, off-whites, and muted blues, evoking a sense of isolation and mystery in this [SETTING]. --ar 9:16
```

---

## Variables

| Variable | Description | Examples |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| `[SUBJECT]` | The main focus of the scene | dilapidated cabin, solitary figure, strange totem, twisted tree |
| `[MOOD]` | Emotional atmosphere | eerie, melancholy, mysterious, haunting, peaceful |
| `[COLOR_SCHEME]` | Color palette | monochromatic blues, warm earth tones, cool grays, deep blacks and icy blues |
| `[SETTING]` | Environment/location | forest clearing, desolate mountain path, barren tundra, shadowed glen |
